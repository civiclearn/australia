Questions about preparing for the citizenship test

How long does access last?

Access is valid for life. You pay once and can come back to practise whenever you like.

Are these the exact questions from the official test?

The government does not publish the exact test questions. Our content is based on the official topics and reflects the types of questions commonly asked in the Australian Citizenship Test.

Is there an official list of questions to memorise?

No single official list exists. Questions can cover Australian history, government, law and values, which is why broad and structured preparation is essential.

How does the platform help me in practice?

With topic-based tests, challenging questions, repetition and score tracking, you gradually strengthen your knowledge and confidence for the test.

Do I need to install an app?

No. The platform works directly in your browser on your phone, tablet or computer.

What if I don’t have time to study every day?

Quick tests and your progress history help you stay consistent, even with limited time. You can immediately see which topics need more work.
